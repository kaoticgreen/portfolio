Student Writing: Relic Cinematic

Project Overview

This student sample demonstrates my ability to write a cinematic or cutscene with humor, good pacing, and a solid hook at the end to pique the player's interest in playing the game.

Key Components

  • Humor: The player was going to spend a lot of time with the AI helper, Gimbal, so I wanted to use humor in the Cinematic to bond the two characters.
  • Inciting Incident: I literally used a gravitational wave to pull the player into the Derelict that they're going to explore.
  • Hook: I used quick pacing to connect the player's local inciting incident with the global conflict represented by the derelict ship, which is a planet killer.

Excerpt

[PANEL] Sam gets up from the console, stretching, and starts walking toward the flight deck.
SAM WARD
(Stretching, responding to Gimbal's sarcasm) You had one job to do.
GIMBAL
(Covering her ass) I was busy... scanning the area.
[PANEL] Sam reaches the flight console and glances back toward Gimbal's camera.
SAM WARD
(Calling out Gimbal) Alotta good that did me.
GIMBAL
(Slightly embarrassed but keeping a poker face) No comment.
