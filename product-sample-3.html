Senior Technical Writer

  • Information Architecture: Ran open and closed card sort exercises, interviewed representatives from every team on Unreal Engine, utilized ANOVA to analyze the company-wide card sort results, and leveraged A/B testing to design documentation information architecture for the launch of UE5.
