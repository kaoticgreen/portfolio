Seeds of Change

Project Overview

This sample demonstrates my ability to create a cast of characters, modify a well known setting, and leverage my knowledge of structure to create a plot that keeps a reader's attention throughout the length of the novel. The goal of my personal project, "Seeds of Change," was to write my first graphic novel so that I could adapt my knowledge of structure and form into a longform narrative that I can eventually turn into a game setting.

  • Read the Book

Key Narrative Components

  • 5 Act Structure: I constructructed the overall story arc using a 5 Act structure to move my characters through the inciting incident, complication, crisis, climax, and resolution.
  • Kishotenketsu: Inspired by Manga, I leveraged the ancient Chinese poetic form of Kishotenketsu to invent new twists and keep the story interesting within a scene.
  • Jo Ha Kyu: Inspired by interpretive dance, I constructed the aesthetics of each scene using the aesthetic philosophy of Jo Ha Kyu in order to build a unique environment that constantly changes for my characters to grapple with.

Excerpt from Issue 9: A Moment on the Bridge

This sample demonstrates narrative scripting for my graphic novel, showing how I use voice-over to build character and backstory during a quiet moment over the Hope Memorial Bridge in Cleveland.

[PANEL 1] We cut to an exterior shot of the ambulance, which is situated in the center of the column that is being led by the tank. This should be a bird's eye view of the convoy crossing the Hope Memorial Bridge over the Cuyahoga River.
SAL'S VOICE OVER
You know what, Doc. I can't believe Kowalski's layin' here like a vegetable.
[PANEL 2] We see one of the Guardians of Transportation looking over Cleveland as Sal continues his voice over.
SAL'S VOICE OVER
Back at the Academy, he kept tellin' us how great it was to grow up in Cleveland. He was proud to be from the city where Eliott Ness took on the mafia.
[PANEL 3] Cut to a shot of the convoy over the bridge as Sal keeps reminiscing.
SAL'S VOICE OVER
Oh yeah, "The Untouchables," that's what it was. Anyway, when we discovered Dias' connection to the mob, it was this man, a proud son of Cleveland, who put it all on the line...

Conclusion

This sample highlights my commitment to complete a novel, which was an experience that leveled up my general storytelling abilities. To keep track of the different throughlines, I wrote the script with Scrivener.
