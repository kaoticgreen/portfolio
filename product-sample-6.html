Paragon

Editor

Key Contributions

  • Character Biographies: Edited and refined lore entries for heroes such as Narbash and Countess to deepen player investment.
  • World Consistency: Maintained the tone and style of the "Agora" universe, ensuring new heroes fit seamlessly into the existing mythology.
