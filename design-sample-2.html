Unreal Editor for Fortnite (Talisman)

Project Overview

This sample demonstrates my strong ability to communicate project goals and vision across disciplines, collaborate with engineers, artists, and designers, and provide narrative direction to deliver engaging marketing, documentation, and in-game assets that enrich the user's experience with this multi-faceted template project. The goal of this project was to ship multiple templates that show developers how to ship realistic assets, characters, and environments with Unreal Engine, Unreal Editor for Fortnite, MetaHuman Creator, and Marvelous Designer

Key Deliverables

  • Communication: Communicated with executive stakeholders, department leads, and across business lines to keep the project on track for a successful launch.
  • Collaboration: Collaborated with dozens of engineers, designers, and artists to ensure that the templates could be played on everything from a Nintendo Switch device to a high-end PC with all of the graphics settings set to Epic quality.
  • Narrative Direction: Designed a narrator to provide a tour of Talisman, giving both players and developers an immersive learning experience in a science fiction setting.
