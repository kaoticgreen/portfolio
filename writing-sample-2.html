Paragon

Project Overview

This sample demonstrates my skill in **environmental storytelling** and writing concise, evocative **flavor text** for in-game assets. The goal was to build a cohesive world history through small details and scattered texts, enriching the player's experience without reliance on heavy exposition.

Key Deliverables

  • **Item Descriptions:** Wrote compelling descriptions for over 50 unique items (weapons, consumables, artifacts).
  • **Environmental Logs:** Developed short journal entries, maintenance logs, and warning signs placed throughout level geometry.
  • **Style Guide Adherence:** Maintained a consistent tone, glossary, and lexicon established in the project's high-level narrative design documents.

Excerpt: Item Description

This is an example of a flavor text entry for a low-tier relic found early in the game:

**Worn Lockbox Key:** A small, brass key, strangely warm to the touch. The teeth are filed down from centuries of use, yet the simple hinge-pattern is recognizable as pre-Collapse construction. *It doesn't open anything here, but you feel a sense of strange anticipation holding it.*

Conclusion

Effective lore writing requires not just creativity, but technical discipline. This sample highlights the ability to deliver narrative impact within strict character limits and technical constraints. The full document includes a searchable database of all created in-game text assets.
