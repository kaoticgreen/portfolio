Relic

Project Overview

This sample demonstrates my ability to lead a narrative team on an award-winning student project. As the Narrative Lead for "Relic," I was responsible for delivering all narrative elements for both the student film and the accompanying game project. My goal was to ensure the story was woven into the gameplay, not just presented in cutscenes.

Key Design Contributions

  • Narrative Leadership: I wrote the cinematic and game scripts, directed the voice actors, and managed another writer's deliverables to ensure a cohesive story.
  • Cross-Discipline Collaboration: Worked directly with audio designers and music composers to organize and integrate all narrative assets, including writing lyrics for the "Zaxonian War Cries" orchestral song.
  • Character Design: Developed the core characters (Sam and Gimbal) and their relationship, which was recognized by DigiPen, winning Best Spoken Dialogue and Best Characters.
  • World Building: Created a 5,000-year story bible and chronology for the "Toncrich Galactic Sector". This included star charts, planetary system maps, and unique counting systems for the three alien species.
  • In-Game Narrative Systems: Designed and scripted narrative events and collectible relics that reward player exploration with lore, including three distinct musical relics for each of the game's alien species.

Game Script Excerpt

This snippet from the game script occurs after the player finds the main objective. It shows a major turning point that demonstrates character, stakes, and the integration of narrative with gameplay.

[PANEL] Gimbal stops Sam in the hallway as they are trying to escape.
GIMBAL
(Authoritative) Sam!
SAM WARD
(Annoyed) What?!
GIMBAL
(Cautionary) We... can't leave yet.
SAM WARD
(Stupefied) You crazy?!
GIMBAL
(Horrified) The ship's neural cluster... it's targeting Zaxon Prime!
SAM WARD
(Horrified) Did we...
GIMBAL
(Afraid) We did.
SAM WARD
(Inquisitive) How do we stop this thing?!
GIMBAL
(Directional) Follow me. I think I know a way.
