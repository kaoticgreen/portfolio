Melee Combat System Pitch

Document Purpose & Philosophy

This document served as the foundational **Game Design Document (GDD) subsection** for the combat system of a third-person action game. It focuses on the feel, control fidelity, and core mechanics required to deliver a satisfying, skill-based melee experience for the player.

Focus: Player Agency, Animation Blending, and State Management.

Core System Mechanics

The system is built on a simple attack chain with deep defensive options:

  • **Simple Combo System:** Light and Heavy attacks can be chained for varying damage/effects.
  • **Parry & Riposte:** A high-risk, high-reward mechanic that requires precise timing to stun opponents.
  • **Stamina Management:** A resource system balancing offensive and defensive actions to encourage tactical play.

Key Design Excerpt: Parry Window

A successful parry requires the player to activate the defense action within a **6-frame window** immediately prior to the enemy's attack connection. Failure to time the parry results in a standard block (if a shield is equipped) or a full hit and stagger.

"The emotional moment of the parry is key: the short window ensures mastery is rewarded, while the high payoff (the riposte) makes the risk feel worth it. The system is designed to reward observation over button mashing."
